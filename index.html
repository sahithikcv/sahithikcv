Featured Project

IndicDLP: A Foundational Dataset for Document Layout Parsing

Published at ICDAR 2025 (Best Student Paper Runner-up)

[Project Teaser Image: Dataset Overview]

Abstract

IndicDLP is the largest manual layout dataset for document understanding in Indian languages. It contains over 121K+ images, 42 region labels, and 12 domains across 11 languages plus English. Our work establishes new baselines for multilingual robustness in layout parsing and structural reconstruction.
